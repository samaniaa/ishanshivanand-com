Few mid-sized stars — never a field of uniform specks. Each star is a bright core (2–3px) wrapped in a tight soft halo, so it reads as a star, not a dot: 8 bright stars across the crown plus 6 faint accents for depth, warm-white (#ffe9c4) and cool-white (#eef2ff) mixed. They keep to the upper sky, thinning toward the warm horizon. The key move: each star twinkles on its own rhythm — an independent 2–4s opacity pulse with a staggered delay, so the field shimmers gently rather than blinking in unison.
