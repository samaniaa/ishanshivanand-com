Few mid-sized stars — never a field of uniform specks. Each star is a bright core (≈1.5–2.4px) wrapped in a soft glow halo (≈5–9px), so it reads as a star, not a dot: 6 hero stars plus 3 faint accents (nine in all), warm-white and cool-white mixed. They stay in the upper sky — masked out below the horizon line so they never litter the page — and breathe on a slow ~7s brightness pulse: the twinkle is a gentle glow, never a blink. Rendered by .day-sky__stars; shown only in the dark phases (pre-dawn, blue hour, twilight, post-dusk).
