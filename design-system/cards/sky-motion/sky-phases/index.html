Never blackThe day is bookended by light — warm blush at pre-dawn, silvery afterglow at post-dusk. Always a sign of light.
Blue & blushBlue carries the day; blush edges every transition — the signature of a rising and setting sun.
Brand navy crownThe dark phases crown in --c-dark-anchor/--c-dark-lift so the light story stays cohesive with the brand.
Fire = orange ballAt daybreak and sundown the sky is ablaze and the sun is an orange disc on the horizon (white overhead at midday).
SymmetryEach morning phase mirrors an evening twin around midday: pre-dawn↔post-dusk, blue hour↔twilight, sunrise↔dusk, daybreak↔sundown, morning gold↔evening gold, morning↔afternoon.
