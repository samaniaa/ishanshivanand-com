Brand · locked design rules

Source of truthAarti’s Framer mock + her copy. No invented structure.
No ornamentNo decorative numbering (no “01”, no Roman numerals). No scattered seal/laurel watermarks.
One link stylePill buttons + plain text links. No arrow/dash links, no “Explore” labels. Labels are plain verbs.
The dayOne continuous sky scrubbed by scroll drives sky, light, range, and ink together. Ink flips only over empty sky (the sky-breath spacers), never over text. See the Sky Scale card + SKY.md.
Sky is never blackThe day is bookended by light — warm blush at pre-dawn, silvery afterglow at post-dusk. Blue carries the day, blush edges every rising/setting sun, brand navy crowns the dark phases. A page may run its own slice of the arc but obeys these laws.
Motto bookends“Rise and Lift Others” opens pre-dawn and closes at post-dusk. No seal in the page flow — the close is motto + values + CTA only.
RegistersCoat of arms + seal → certificate / brand-mark register only (favicon, credentials), never placed in a page section. Monastery story → closing About block only.
MenuRight-side glass drawer, parents only — children live on landing pages.

These rules are locked by the designer-owner. Present whole design plans, not piecemeal questions.
