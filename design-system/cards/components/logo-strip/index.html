The “featured in / trusted by” treatment, built to sit on any page’s ending sky — a gold .eyebrow above a centred .logo-strip of real press logos. Because every page closes on a different sky, the strip has a light and a dark version: --on-dark colours the logos warm-white for dark skies, --on-light brand navy (--c-dark-anchor) for light skies — one logo set, either sky. Each logo is a monochrome silhouette used as a mask, tinted by currentColor, so the colour is exact (not a filtered approximation). Normalised to one eyeline by height (--tall / --wide tune odd aspects), resting at opacity:.85. On a real page, supply the art via a --logo mask or an inline SVG with fill:currentColor.
