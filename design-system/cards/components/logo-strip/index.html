Trusted by

The “trusted by / featured in” treatment from the book page: a gold .eyebrow label (--gold) centred above a .logo-strip. The strip is a plain centred flex row — logos normalised to one eyeline by height (--tall / --wide tune odd aspects), resting at opacity:.85, lifting to full on hover. The logos carry the colour: supply monochrome art, or add logo-strip--invert to force any logo to flat white on a dark surface (top).
