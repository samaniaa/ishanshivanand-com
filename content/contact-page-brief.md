# Contact page — Claude Design brief

Paste-in brief for the Claude Design canvas. Goal: ideate the look of the Contact
page (`/connect/`) for ishanshivanand.com. Explore **two framings side by side**,
pick or blend, and the resulting `.dc.html` becomes the source of truth I port to
code. Keep it continuous with the homepage and the book page.

---

## The concept it lives inside

The site is "One day, one life": the homepage is a single day scrubbed by scroll,
and the day IS Dr. Ishan Shivanand's biography (pre-dawn to night). One traveling
light (crescent moon to sun to moon) is the signature. Contact is the **last stop
in the journey**, so it can honestly be read two ways. Design both:

### Board A — Night close
The day ending. Deep navy sky, real glowing stars (light blues and lilacs, not
faint specks), the returned crescent moon low in the frame. Reflective, quiet,
generous. Carries the homepage's closing register: the motto "Rise and Lift Others"
and the values line "Compassion · Oneness · Valour". Reaching out at the end of a
full day.

### Board B — First light
Reaching out as a beginning. Pre-dawn navy lifting toward a warm sunrise horizon
band ("blue hour"), the crescent moon high and dimming as light gathers. Hopeful,
open, forward. "Begin a conversation" as the start of a new day.

Both boards use the same content and form. Only the light, palette temperature, and
closing note change. We decide by looking.

---

## Intent and headline

- Working intent: **Begin a conversation.**
- The page invites enquiries and routes them by interest. It should feel personal
  and unhurried, not a corporate "Contact Us" grid.

## The five interest routes

Present these as the routing structure (cards or a quiet list, per the design). Each
is a reason someone writes in. A general office line sits beneath them.

1. **Book, writing, and forewords** — Forewords, endorsements, translation rights,
   and writing collaborations around *The Practice of Immortality* and new work.
2. **Research and academic** — Peer-reviewed studies, university partnerships, and
   academic collaboration on the mental-health protocols.
3. **Advisory and corporate** — Advisory engagements and corporate mental-health
   and performance programs for teams and leadership.
4. **Speaking and appearances** — Keynotes, panels, and appearances for
   conferences, campuses, and convenings.
5. **Media and press** — Interviews, press enquiries, and editorial requests.

General: for anything else, the office. `office@ishanshivanand.com` (destination
still to be confirmed by Aarti — treat as placeholder).

## The form

A calm, single-column enquiry form, not a dense field grid.

- **Fields**: Name · Email · Interest (a select carrying the five routes above) ·
  Message.
- **Submit**: "Send enquiry."
- **Success state**: a warm, personal thank-you that replaces the form after send
  ("Thank you. The office will be in touch."). Design this state too.
- The endpoint is wired later, so the form only needs to look and feel finished.

## Design system

Use the synced **"Ishan Shivanand — Design System"** project as the source of
truth for palette, gradients, type (Source Serif 4 display / Inter body), the
fluid scale, spacing, buttons, and the semantic ink tokens. Do not re-specify any
of that here. This brief only adds the page-specific intent below.

The only page-level behavior to hold from the system: **ink stays in sync with the
sky** — warm-white ink on the dark board, navy ink on the light/dawn board, gold as
accent. See the semantic-tokens and sky cards.

## Reference anchors (keep it continuous)

- **Homepage night close**: the day ends on a gold seal, the motto "Rise and Lift
  Others", and "Compassion · Oneness · Valour", over brand navy. Board A should feel
  like the same evening.
- **The crescent moon** is the site signature (moon → sun → moon). It belongs on
  both boards: low and returned on the night board, high and dimming on the dawn
  board.
- **Book-page night sky**: glowing stars + a soft, small crescent moon with a gentle
  glow (a quiet companion, not a beacon). Same treatment here.
- **One link system**: navy pill buttons (they flip warm-white on dark) plus
  gold-underline text links. Nothing else.

## Design rules (see the brand/rules card)

The system's `brand/rules` card governs. The reminders most likely to bite on this
page: no decorative numbering, one link system (pill buttons + gold-underline text
links, nothing else), no scattered seals, and mind the spacing between a header and
its content. Keep it serene and luminous even on the night board, never heavy.

## Voice (for any copy on the canvas)

No em dashes. American spelling. "USA Today", never NYT. Avoid the "not X but Y"
construction. Warm, plain, and dignified.
