Peer-reviewed research validates his mental health protocols, adopted at Mayo Clinic, Google, Amazon, and MD Anderson. He has been recognized by the US Congress, received by UK Parliament, and invited to the White House Office of National Drug Control Policy roundtable on the opioid crisis.
